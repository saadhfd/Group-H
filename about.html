Your local travel experts in the Nordic region

Nordic Visitor is a specialist travel agency catering to travellers who want an original, authentic and unforgettable experience in the North.

The Nordic Visitor destinations must be seen to be believed. Picture the striking glaciers and waterfalls of Iceland, the shimmering fjords and lakes of Scandinavia, the rugged Irish coastline, the scenic countryside and historic sights of the British Isles, the towering peaks of the Alps and the remote communities of Lapland and Greenland. You have a lot to look forward to!

Since Nordic Visitor was established in 2002, we have had the pleasure of welcoming tens of thousands of happy travellers from over 80 countries to our destinations. We hope to be able to welcome you and introduce you to our beautiful and fascinating regions we are so proud to call home.

 
 

Our values

Reasons to book with us

Personal

We operate on a first name basis here. You will have your own custom-made itinerary and a dedicated travel consultant who will serve as your point of contact before, during and after your trip.

Quality

Nordic Visitor is certified by Vakinn, the official quality label for Icelandic tourism, and our commitment to professional staff development has been recognised with an education award in Iceland.

Trust

Travel in confidence! Nordic Visitor is fully licensed by the Icelandic Tourist Board and is a member of various professional associations. We’re also highly rated on Tripadvisor by our travellers.

Dedication

We know your time is valuable, so our team is committed to providing you with the best travel experience possible, from before you book your holiday to after your journey has ended.

Nordic Visitor is staffed by a team of highly motivated and experienced travel professionals who know our European destinations inside and out. Feel free to contact a member of our team from the directory below to field any questions you might have.
